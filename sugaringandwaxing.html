Sugaring 

Sugaring is a form of hair removal that dates back to the civilizations of ancient Mesopotamia, Egypt, and Greece. The sugar paste we use is made solely of sugar, water, and citric acid. It’s completely organic and all natural. Because there are no harsh chemicals, this is an excellent option for those with sensitive skin, those who are prone to skin irritation, or simply those who advocate for a more natural approach to life. Sugar paste is water soluble so it won’t stick to the skin. Leaving the live skin cells intact means less irritation for your beautiful skin! 

Pros of sugaring:
-Clients who prefer sugaring say that sugaring hurts less than waxing.
-The melting point of sugar is lower than wax, therefore it does not feel as hot on your skin.
-Sugaring has the ability to remove much shorter hair than waxing–about 1/8 of an inch.

Waxing
Why get waxed by the rest?
You should get waxed by the best!

Waxing is hair removal that uses soy wax to remove body hair from its root so it lasts longer than shaving. This process gives your skin a wonderful exfoliation, removing dead and lifeless layers of skin and promoting cell turnover. Waxing can last up to 2 weeks before new hair grows back. To wax again, you need to wait until the new hairs grow 1/4 inch otherwise the wax will not be able to grab the hairs to remove hair.

At East Hill Massage and Aesthetics we proudly use all natural and organic soy wax that is enriched with Vitamin E and other wholesome nutrients to maximize hair removal and protect the skin.
